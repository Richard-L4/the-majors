The Founders

Tom Morris

'Old Tom Morris' was born on June 16th 1821 and died on May 24th 1908. He won the championship four times in 186, 1862, 1864 and 1867.

The Claret Jug

The Claret Jug is the trophy awarded to the winner of The Open Championship, made of Sterling Silver, first awarded in 1872.

Allan Robertson

Allan Robertson regarded as being the first 'Club Professional' was born on December the 11th 1815 and died on March 1st 1859.
